About

Hello , I am Syamini Kaushik. I work as a concept artist and illustrator based in India. My focus is in Character design and portraiture for films and videogames.

I am currently completing my Bachelors at Image College of Arts and Technology (Bangalore) in Visual Communication - Digital media, 3D Animation.

In the field of drawing, painting and sculpture, I am mostly self-taught. I have an amply strong foundation in traditional media. As I am always indulged in numerous different art forms and experimenting with all sorts of mediums, I have tried to work towards developing an adaptable client-centric art style.

I have done a few small freelance projects: mostly commissioned sketches and fan art, posters, book and album covers; and have worked as a clean-up artist for a small newspaper comic strip.

Work contained on this site is what I do in my leisure time.
